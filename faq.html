FAQ

How can I buy something from the HZ Webshop?

If you click here you will be directed to the HZ Webshop, then you just need to sign in with your HZ account, once you have done that you can now buy what you want.

What informations can I find in MyHZ?

In MyHZ you can find many useful informations, like your timetable, as well as it's changes, you can see your study progress, and you can also access Learn, so you can view your courses and programmes.

Where can I park my car near HZ in Middelburg?

Unfortunately you can't park your car in HZ, but luckily you can park on the car park of PZEM at the Poelendaelesingel 10 for free.

How can I print a document from my laptop at HZ?

You need to go to the HZ print page, log into your account and then you can upload all the files you want to print. After choosing the file(s) you can set additional settings and confirm your print request. Next, you go to the printer (they are on each floor), scan your card in it, choose the printing option and just wait until your document is printed.

I have COVID / COVID symptoms. What do I need to do?

All the information about COVID can be found in this site.

How can I book a classroom in HZ?

When you go to the Self Service Portal, select "Make a reservation", followed by "Reserve meeting room or Project Group room" and then "Reserve a Room", after that you can just pick an available time and enjoy your room.
